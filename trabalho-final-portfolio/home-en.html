SENIOR ANDROID DEVELOPER

Summary of My Journey

I am grateful for the incredible opportunity for growth and learning as an Android Developer. I have been working since 2018, in 2023 I complete 5 years of experience.

I deal with OOP (Object Oriented Programming), Java, Kotlin. I consume Rest APIs. Version control by Git. Access control and user session control, manipulation of views and customized components, Android Studio (IDE), programming logic, libraries from the Jetpack package, reactive programming (using livedata, rxjava, coroutines), design patterns, SOLID principles, user permissions, dependency management with gradle and maven, navigation between activities, fragments, modules and apps, creation of complex layouts, architecture of modular and non-modular projects with MVVM, MVI, Clean Architecture, performance, security, dependency injection with Koin, Dagger and Hilt, bug fixes, CD/CI automation, agility with Kanban and Scrum, unit tests and user experience.

Since January/2023 I have been working on the core team of the Super App Magalu. With millions of users, with a huge volume of monthly transactions, widely used and downloaded in the app store. My team focuses on improving the development experience and performance, maintainability, organization, simplification and decoupling. I work on tasks related to standardization of architecture, modularization, refactoring, updating libraries, dependency management, maintenance, code review, cultivation of good programming practices, correction, testing and implementation of new features according to market strategies.

From May/2021 to January/2023 I had the opportunity to participate in a project for one of the largest Brazilian banks, Bradesco, with a focus on security and performance, working as a Senior Android Developer, by NTT DATA a multinational company with more than 26 thousand employees and presence in 17 countries.

From November 2020 to May/2021 I worked on the development of a library that uses Blockchain concepts at CPQD. An innovative idea that proposes to improve the way of validating identity and access to data respecting the Brazilian General Data Protection Law.

From December/2019 to October/2020 I participated in the development of a Framework with concepts of ServerDriverUI, DeclarativeViews and Flexbox called Beagle, in the products team of Zup Innovation, which provided me with new knowledge in Android and also added Backend knowledge.

I practice daily and seek to deepen my knowledge in Mobile Development and related subjects.
