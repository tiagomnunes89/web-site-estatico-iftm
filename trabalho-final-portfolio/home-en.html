Summary of My Journey

I am grateful for the incredible opportunity to grow and learn as an Android Developer. I started working in this field in 2018, and by 2023, I had amassed five years of experience.

My skills include Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), Java, Kotlin, consuming Rest APIs, and version control with Git. I handle access control and user session control, manipulate views and custom components, and work with Android Studio (IDE). I'm proficient in programming logic, libraries from the Jetpack package, reactive programming (using LiveData, RxJava, Coroutines), design patterns, SOLID principles, user permissions, and dependency management with Gradle and Maven. I navigate between activities, fragments, modules, and apps, create complex layouts, and work on the architecture of modular and non-modular projects with MVVM, MVI, Clean Architecture. I focus on performance, security, dependency injection with Koin, Dagger, and Hilt, bug fixes, CD/CI automation, agility with Kanban and Scrum, unit tests, and user experience.

Since January 2023, I have been part of the core team of the Super App Magalu, a widely used and downloaded app in the app store with millions of users and a high volume of monthly transactions. Our team focuses on improving the development experience, performance, maintainability, organization, simplification, and decoupling. I work on tasks related to the standardization of architecture, modularization, refactoring, updating libraries, dependency management, maintenance, code review, promoting good programming practices, correction, testing, and implementing new features according to market strategies.

From May 2021 to January 2023, I had the opportunity to participate in a project for one of Brazil's largest banks, Bradesco. I focused on security and performance, working as a Senior Android Developer for NTT DATA, a multinational company with over 26,000 employees and a presence in 17 countries.

From November 2020 to May 2021, I worked on the development of a library that uses Blockchain concepts at CPQD. This innovative idea aims to improve the way of validating identity and accessing data in compliance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law.

From December 2019 to October 2020, I participated in the development of a Framework with concepts of ServerDriverUI, DeclarativeViews, and Flexbox called Beagle, in the products team of Zup Innovation. This experience provided me with new knowledge in Android and also added Backend knowledge.

I practice daily and strive to deepen my knowledge in Mobile Development and related subjects.
