Summary of My Journey

Senior Android Developer with over 6 years of experience building scalable, high-performance apps with a strong focus on user experience.

I currently work at PayPal (via Taller), collaborating with global teams on one of the world’s leading payment apps. My role involves test automation, code quality, and continuous delivery in a collaborative environment where all communication is conducted in professional English.

Over the years, I’ve contributed to large-scale projects across fintech, retail, innovation, and digital identity, working with companies like Magazine Luiza, Bradesco (via NTT DATA), CPQD, and Zup Innovation. Throughout these experiences, I’ve applied technologies such as Kotlin and Java, used the Jetpack libraries and Android SDK, implemented modern architectures, automated testing, and worked within agile development practices.

I believe in simple, well-structured solutions built through teamwork. I’m always looking to grow, learn, and deliver value — both to the team and to the end user.
